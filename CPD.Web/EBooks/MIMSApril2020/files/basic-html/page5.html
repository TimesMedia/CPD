        How to Use Mims



        Medicine information in MIMS is updated monthly and is compiled from package inserts supplied by pharmaceutical companies
        and should be read in conjunction with Standard Editorial Notes as well as Information for Prescribers. All MIMS entries follow the
        same format, the interpretation of which, though entirely fictitious, follows in this new format. Entries printed in red indicate new
        product introductions that have accompanying advertising support. These entries are compiled strictly according to their respective
        package insert in line with the MIMS editorial criteria.
